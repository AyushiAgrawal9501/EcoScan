How it works
01
Image capture
Your camera frame or uploaded file is drawn onto an HTML5 Canvas, converting it into raw pixel data the model can read.
02
On-device AI
TensorFlow.js runs your trained Convolutional Neural Network entirely in the browser — no image ever leaves your device.
03
Classification
The model outputs probability scores for each class (Organic, Recyclable…). The highest confidence score wins.
04
CO₂ impact
The predicted class maps to lifecycle CO₂ data, showing exactly how much emissions you prevent by disposing correctly.
